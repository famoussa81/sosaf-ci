Quality

Certifications and quality control

SOSAF-CI works in collaboration with agricultural cooperatives and partner packing stations holding recognized certifications, notably GlobalG.A.P. for certain products such as mango and coconut.

Through this network of certified partners, we are able to offer products that comply with international market requirements, with enhanced monitoring of quality, traceability and packaging before export.

  • Quality control before each shipment
  • Compliance with international export standards and norms
  • Full product traceability, from field to container
  • Provision of required documentation per order

Frequently asked questions

Everything about SOSAF-CI

Find answers to the most common questions about our products, export process and services.

SOSAF-CI exports a wide range of tropical fruits from Ivory Coast: mango (Kent, Amélie), coconut (fresh and dried), avocado (Hass), banana (Cavendish) and pineapple (MD2, Smooth Cayenne). We can also supply other agricultural products depending on seasonal availability.

We work in collaboration with agricultural cooperatives and partner packing stations holding GlobalG.A.P. certification, particularly for mango and coconut. This network of certified partners enables us to offer products that comply with international market requirements, with rigorous monitoring of quality, traceability and packaging.

We are flexible and can fulfill both full container orders and pallet or negotiated volume orders. Our network of growers and cooperatives allows us to ensure continuous supply and adapt to your specific needs. Contact us to discuss the quantities you are interested in.

We provide all documents required for export: commercial invoice, packing list, phytosanitary certificate, certificate of origin, transport documents, and quality certificates as available. We ensure full transparency on all documents accompanying your order.

We offer two transport modes: sea freight (refrigerated containers for large volumes, 10–20 days depending on destination) and air freight (for urgent orders or small volumes, 2–5 days). The choice of transport mode depends on your destination, order volume and urgency. We ship to Europe, the Middle East, Asia and the Americas.

Each product is professionally packaged, adapted to its nature and destination: ventilated cartons for mangoes with protective nets, bags or crates for coconuts, cell-pack cartons for avocados, lined cartons for bananas and pineapples. All packaging is designed to preserve freshness and comply with your country's import standards.

SOSAF-CI exports to all international markets: Europe (France, Netherlands, Belgium, Germany, Spain…), Middle East (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar…), Asia and the Americas. We adapt our logistics and documentation to the specific requirements of each destination market.

You can contact us directly by email or WhatsApp (details below) with your requirements: desired products, volumes, destination and timelines. Our team will respond within 24 hours with a detailed commercial proposal, prices and logistics conditions tailored to your request.

Yes, we can arrange to send samples to allow you to evaluate the quality of our products before placing an order. Sample shipping conditions and timelines are to be discussed with our sales team — contact us to learn more.
